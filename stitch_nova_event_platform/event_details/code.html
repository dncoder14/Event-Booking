About the Event

Prepare for an unprecedented sensory journey where the raw energy of underground synthesizers meets the soaring elegance of a full chamber orchestra. Neon Symphony: Vol. 4 is the latest installment in Nocturne's signature immersive series, curated for those who find their pulse in the dark.

Set within the brutalist architecture of the Void Warehouse, the event features 360-degree spatial audio and a reactive light installation that breathes with the music. This isn't just a concert—it's a digital nocturne designed to blur the lines between reality and simulation.
